    About us

    About NeptuneX Game Studio

    At NeptuneX Game Studio, we are dedicated to creating a wide range of mobile gaming experiences that captivate and entertain players around the world. Based in Kyiv, our talented team of developers and artists brings passion and innovation to every project, from hyper-casual games to more complex and immersive titles.

    We strive to push the boundaries of creativity, ensuring that each game we produce is visually stunning, engaging, and memorable. Whether it's a quick play or a deep dive, NeptuneX is committed to delivering quality games that appeal to a broad audience.

    Our Philosophy

    At NeptuneX Game Studio, our vision is to be at the forefront of mobile gaming, creating experiences that not only entertain but inspire players worldwide. We aim to blend innovation with creativity, developing games that resonate with diverse audiences and stand the test of time.

    Our goal is to push the boundaries of what's possible in mobile gaming, delivering high-quality, immersive, and unforgettable experiences. By fostering a culture of collaboration and continuous learning, we strive to be a leader in the industry, setting new standards for excellence in game development.
